Why · What · How

Why This Exists

The Problem is Real & Painful

3M+ independent personal trainers globally are running their businesses on WhatsApp, Google Sheets, and paper notebooks. They lose clients because they forget follow-ups, lose revenue because they can't track payments, and burn out because admin eats 2–3 hours per day. Existing tools like Trainerize or PT Distinction cost $40–130/month and are bloated desktop-first products not built for a trainer who is always on the move with a phone.

What You're Building

A Mobile-First Business OS for PTs

FitDesk is a mobile-first client management platform — not just a workout builder. It handles the full PT business loop: onboard clients, write workout & nutrition plans, track client progress, collect payments, send automated check-ins, and get business analytics. Everything a PT needs to run a professional, growing business from their phone — in under 5 minutes per client per week.

How You Win

Mobile-First + Price Disruption

You win by being genuinely mobile-native (competitors are web apps with bad mobile UX), pricing aggressively ($9–19/mo vs $40–130/mo), and shipping fast as a solo dev. Your MERN + TypeScript stack lets you build the backend API fast, React Native gives you iOS + Android from one codebase. You don't need to be better than everyone in every way — you just need to be the best mobile experience at the lowest price. That gap is wide open.

Tech Stack & Architecture

📱

React Native + Expo

Single codebase for iOS + Android. Expo handles OTA updates, push notifications, camera access for progress photos. TypeScript throughout. React Navigation v6 for routing.

⚙️

Node.js + Express + TypeScript

REST API backend. You already know this. JWT auth, role-based middleware, structured error handling. Modular route/controller/service pattern for clean scaling.

🗄️

MongoDB + Mongoose

Flexible schema fits workout/nutrition plan data perfectly. Atlas free tier for dev. Indexes on trainerId + clientId for fast queries. GridFS or S3 for progress photos.

💳

Stripe Billing

Stripe Subscriptions API for your SaaS billing. Stripe Connect for PT-to-client payment collection (Phase 2). Webhooks for payment events. Stripe Customer Portal for self-service.

🔔

FCM + Bull Queue (Redis)

Firebase Cloud Messaging for push notifications. Bull + Redis for background jobs: weekly check-in reminders, payment due alerts, scheduled report generation.

☁️

Railway / Render + S3 + Cloudflare

Railway for Node API (free tier → $5/mo). AWS S3 for progress photo storage. Cloudflare for CDN + DDoS. Total infra cost: ~$10–20/mo at early stage. Zero investment needed.

💡 Zero Investment Reality: Railway free tier + MongoDB Atlas free tier + Expo free tier + Stripe (pay per transaction) = $0 upfront cost to launch. Only pay as you earn. Your Apple Developer account ($99/yr) and Google Play ($25 one-time) are the only mandatory costs.

Phase 1
MVP — Core Business Loop
Weeks 1–10 · ~2.5 months
Goal: First 50 paying users

Build the smallest version that solves the core pain: a PT can onboard clients, assign workout plans, track progress, and collect payment — all from their phone. Nothing more. Ship this fast, get real PTs using it, and charge from day one. Validate before you over-build.

  • Profile setup: name, bio, specialization, profile photo
  • Business info: pricing, session types, timezone
  • Email verification flow with resend option
  • Onboarding checklist (add first client, create first plan)
  • JWT access token + refresh token rotation
Client Management
  • Add client: name, email, phone, goals, health notes
  • Client status: Active / Paused / Completed
  • Send client invite link via SMS/WhatsApp/email
  • Client self-onboarding form (PAR-Q health questionnaire)
  • Client list with search + filter by status
  • Client profile view: full history, plans, payments
  • Archive/delete client with data retention
Workout Plan Builder
  • Create plan: name, duration (weeks), days per week
  • Exercise library: 300+ exercises with muscle group tags
  • Search exercises by name / muscle / equipment
  • Add custom exercises with notes + video link
  • Per-exercise: sets, reps, weight, rest time, RPE
  • Duplicate plans + assign to multiple clients
  • Assign plan to client with start date
Progress Tracking
  • Log client workout session (mark exercises complete)
  • Record weight lifted, reps completed per set
  • Body metrics: weight, body fat %, measurements
  • Progress photos: front/back/side with date
  • Client progress chart: weight over time, strength over time
  • PT notes per session (private, client-invisible)
Payments & Billing
  • Stripe Connect integration — PT connects their Stripe account
  • Create packages: sessions × price (e.g. 12 sessions × $600)
  • Send payment request link to client via any channel
  • Client pays via Stripe-hosted checkout (card / Apple Pay)
  • PT receives payout minus platform fee (5% Phase 1)
  • Invoice generation (PDF) auto-sent to client on payment
  • Payment history tab per client
  • Unpaid / overdue alerts for PT
PT Subscription Billing
  • Stripe Subscriptions for FitDesk's own billing
  • 14-day free trial, no card required
  • Upgrade/downgrade plan from app
  • Trial expiry warning at day 7, 12, 14
  • Graceful downgrade on failed payment (read-only mode)
  • Stripe Customer Portal for self-service billing management

Phase 2
Retention & Growth Features
Months 3–6 · ~3 months
Goal: $3,000 MRR · 150 users

Phase 1 gets users in. Phase 2 keeps them. Churn is the killer of SaaS businesses. You add features that PTs use daily — check-ins, nutrition, scheduling — making FitDesk too sticky to cancel. You also add the Client App so clients have a reason to engage, which locks in the PT even harder.

Client Mobile App (Separate App)
  • Client-facing app: view their assigned workout plan
  • Log their own workout sessions between PT sessions
  • View progress charts (weight, lifts)
  • View and upload progress photos
  • Send messages to PT (simple in-app chat)
  • View upcoming session schedule
  • Receive push notifications from PT
Automated Check-ins
  • PT creates weekly check-in form (custom questions)
  • Auto-send to client every Monday (configurable day)
  • Client fills: weight, energy, sleep, adherence rating
  • PT receives aggregated responses in dashboard
  • Highlight clients who missed check-in (at-risk)
  • Check-in history per client with trend visualization
  • PT can comment/reply to client's check-in
Nutrition Tracking
  • PT creates nutrition plan: calories, protein, carbs, fat targets
  • Meal templates: breakfast / lunch / dinner / snacks
  • Client logs daily meals (simple free-text or from template)
  • PT views client's nutrition compliance rate
  • Weekly nutrition adherence summary in check-in
  • Water intake tracking goal
Session Scheduling
  • PT sets available time slots (recurring weekly schedule)
  • Client books session from available slots
  • Google Calendar / Apple Calendar sync (iCal export)
  • Session reminders: 24h + 1h before (push + email)
  • PT can cancel/reschedule with notification to client
  • Upcoming sessions widget on PT dashboard
  • Session history log per client
In-App Messaging
  • Direct message thread: PT ↔ Client
  • Text messages with read receipts
  • Photo/video attachment support (progress media)
  • Push notification on new message
  • PT can message all clients simultaneously (broadcast)
  • Message templates: "Great work today!", "Check-in reminder"
PT Dashboard & Analytics
  • Monthly revenue chart (collected vs. outstanding)
  • Active client count with churn tracking
  • Client retention rate (3mo, 6mo)
  • Total sessions delivered this month
  • Client engagement score (check-ins completed, workouts logged)
  • At-risk clients (no check-in in 2 weeks, flagged in red)
  • Upcoming renewals / expiring packages alert

Phase 3
AI Layer + Automation
Months 7–10 · ~3 months
Goal: $6,000 MRR · 300 users

By Month 7 you have real user data — workout logs, check-ins, progress photos, payment patterns. Now you layer AI on top of this data to create features competitors literally cannot ship: AI-generated workout plans from client goals, automated client progress reports, and smart business insights that tell PTs which clients are about to churn. This is your moat.

AI Workout Plan Generator
  • PT inputs: client goal, fitness level, equipment, days/week, injuries
  • Claude/GPT-4o API generates full periodized plan (4–12 weeks)
  • PT reviews + edits generated plan before assigning
  • Exercise substitution: "replace barbell squat with something" → AI suggests
  • Deload week auto-inserted every 4th week
  • Plan regeneration if client feedback is negative
AI Progress Reports
  • Monthly auto-generated client progress report (PDF)
  • AI narrates: "Sarah lost 2.3kg this month, strength increased 15%..."
  • Charts: body weight trend, key lift progression, check-in scores
  • Before/after progress photos side by side
  • AI coaching notes: what's working, what to adjust next month
  • PT reviews before sending; one-tap share to client
  • Shareable client success story card for PT's social media
Smart Client Insights
  • Churn prediction: "Client at 70% churn risk — hasn't logged in 10 days"
  • Engagement score per client (green / amber / red)
  • AI-suggested action: "Send Sarah a motivational message now"
  • Package renewal prediction: "Tom's 12-session pack ends in 3 weeks"
  • Auto-reminder to PT: suggest renewal conversation at right time
  • Client comparison: who progresses fastest (to replicate what works)
AI Nutrition Coach
  • Client describes a meal in plain text → AI calculates macros
  • Food photo scan → AI estimates calories (GPT-4o vision)
  • Weekly nutrition summary auto-generated for PT review
  • AI suggests meal swap: "Replace this meal to hit protein target"
  • Nutrition compliance trend chart per client
Automation Engine
  • Trigger-based automations: "If client misses 2 check-ins → send message"
  • Package expiry automation: 7 days before → send renewal offer
  • Birthday message auto-sent to client
  • Session milestone celebration: "You've done 50 sessions with me! 🎉"
  • PT-configurable automation templates
  • Automation activity log (what was sent, when)
PT Business Insights
  • Revenue forecast: projected MRR next 3 months based on renewals
  • Best-performing service package (by retention + revenue)
  • Average client lifetime value (LTV)
  • Client acquisition trend (new clients per month)
  • Busiest day/time for sessions (optimize scheduling)
  • Exportable business report (PDF) for PT's own records

Go-to-Market Strategy

🎯 Key insight: PTs are hyper-concentrated on Instagram, TikTok, and Reddit (r/personaltraining, r/fitness). They trust other PTs more than any ad. Your GTM is entirely organic and community-driven — zero paid ads needed.
🎁

Beta Outreach (Month 1)

DM 100 PTs on Instagram with 1k–50k followers. Offer lifetime 50% discount for beta feedback. Join r/personaltraining and r/fitness subreddits. Post genuine value posts, not ads. Get 10 beta users who give testimonials.

📹

Content Marketing (Month 2+)

Create short TikTok / Reels showing FitDesk in action: "How I manage 30 clients from my phone." Demo videos of AI plan generation. These go viral in PT communities. No ad budget needed — real utility is the hook.

🤝

PT Influencer Partnerships

Partner with 3–5 PT influencers (10k–100k followers). Offer 30% lifetime affiliate commission. They promote genuinely because they use it and earn recurring income. This is your primary acquisition channel at scale.

🎓

PT Certification Schools

Partner with NASM, ACE, and online PT course providers. Offer newly certified PTs 3 months free. They become power users just as they launch their business. One email to a PT school = hundreds of new users.

📝

SEO Content (Month 3+)

Blog posts targeting: "best personal trainer software 2025", "how to manage PT clients", "personal trainer app free trial". Long-tail keywords with low competition. PT-specific content ranks fast in a narrow niche.

🏷️

App Store Optimization

Keywords: "personal trainer app", "pt client management", "fitness coach software". Request reviews at peak satisfaction moments (after first payment received, after first check-in completes). Reviews are your silent salespeople.
